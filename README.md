They now operate two locations, employ four PGA professionals, and run a state-of-the-art Doppler fitting studio. They carry Titleist, Callaway, TaylorMade, Ping, Cleveland, Cobra, Odyssey, and Under Armour.

## Cold-Call Angles

### 1. "Custom fittings are your highest-value service — can people book them at midnight?"
A serious golfer researches equipment late at night. If they can't schedule a fitting online, they schedule it at Golf Galaxy instead. The demo puts a booking form in front of them the moment they're ready to commit.

### 2. "Your 44-year story is your strongest differentiator — it's barely visible online"
Golf Galaxy doesn't have four named PGA pros and 44 years of history. Koosa does. But if their current web presence doesn't tell that story, it's an invisible advantage. The demo makes it the centerpiece.

### 3. "PGA lesson bookings over the phone is 2005 — it's costing you students"
The demo has a lesson scheduling section. Parents looking for junior lessons, adults wanting to improve — they want to book on their lunch break, not call during business hours. An online lesson scheduler fills the calendar passively.

### 4. "Brand-name shoppers are Googling Titleist and Callaway — are they finding you?"
When someone Googles "Titleist fitter Lexington SC," they should find Koosa first. The schema markup and meta description now tell Google exactly what they carry and where.
