Our Story

Columbia's Golf
Shop Since 1980.

It started with Jimmy Koosa and his wife opening the Weed Hill Driving Range in Irmo in 1980. Over four decades later, Koosa Golf has grown into the Midlands' most trusted golf shop — two locations, four PGA professionals, and the same family passion for the game that started it all.

We're not a big-box store. We're your pro shop — the place where you can talk real golf, get real advice, and leave with equipment that actually works for your body and your swing.
